What are the objectives for these Incubators?

FIRST To prepare you for your role at the URoL workshop you’ll attend.

What role I hear you say? So glad you asked.

We ask that you work as part of a team to produce some output from the meeting. It could be a research project with other postdocs and PI´s, it could be a paper on a topic related to the URoL workshop, it could be advice to the community and NSF on how to involve postdocs, or you might decide to join the writing team with PI´s who are producing papers from the URoL workshops. The slate of options is very open.

SECOND It will be an opportunity to network with other postdocs who have an interest in and are working on cool science in domains in which you are also interested.

THIRD It will give you time and space to reflect on the collaborative, cross-disciplinary and use inspired research future of your own career.

About the Incubators

"Book-end" Incubators: Two incubators (each about 4 hours long) will "book-end" the workshops. The first kick-off incubator will be on April 12th just following the short URoL Orientation meeting. The second, wrap-up incubator will be a closing workshop for Postdocs only, scheduled for May 17th. This one will especially emphasize reflection and networking.

Project/Paper writing Incubators

There will attend one of two project/paper writing Incubators (about 4 hours long). Which one will depend on which URoL workshop you attend; they are scheduled for April 22nd (for attendees of Workshops 1 and 2) and May 2nd (for attendees of Workshops 3 and 4).

We invite you to hold all 4 Incubator dates in your calendar if possible.
