What are the objectives for these Incubators?

FIRST To prepare you for your role at the URoL workshop.

You will be working with a small team of fellow postdocs to produce an output from the meeting. The output will be decided by your group. Possibilities include an outline of a research project with other postdocs and PIÂ´s; a publication-quality manuscript on a scientific topic related to the URoL workshop; a paper giving advice to the community and NSF on how to involve postdocs in the different stages of cross-disciplinary research. These suggestions should not be limiting - decide on what is best for you and your group.

SECOND To network with other postdocs who have an interest in and are working on cool science in domains in which you are also interested.

THIRDTo give you time and space to reflect on the collaborative, cross-disciplinary and use inspired research future of your own career.

About the Incubators

"Book-end" Incubators

Two incubators (each about 4 hours long) will "book-end" the workshops. The first kick-off incubator will be on April 12 following the URoL Workshop Orientation. The second, "wrap-up" incubator will be a closing workshop for Postdocs only, scheduled for May 17. This one will especially emphasize reflection and networking.

Writing Incubators

You will attend one of two project/paper writing Incubators (about 4 hours long). Which one will depend on which URoL workshop you attend; they are scheduled for April 22 (for attendees of Workshops 1 and 2) and May 2 (for attendees of Workshops 3 and 4).

We invite you to hold all 4 Incubator dates in your calendar if possible.
