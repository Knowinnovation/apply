"Catching Methane"

We need your help to accelerate the ways in which we capture Methane.

We know that Methane impacts on climate change faster and with greater severity than CO2. If we are to achieve global targets then we must tackle methane capture with the same or greater focus that we are currently applying to CO2

We are now beyond the point where we need to understand the problem better and we want to move to action. We acknowledge that it this is not just a problem for science - any solution will require inter-disciplinary collaboration between policy-makers, social scientists, economists and engineers.

About the Workshops

We want to make the most effective use of your time so we have divided the workshops into two. The first on (April 7 and 8) will focus on articulating the challenge. We are particularly interested in hearing from engineers and technicians who are working in this field.

The second workshop will focus on developing solutions that can be implemented over the next XX years. We are particularly interested in working with social scientists, policy makers and conomists.

The workshops will run virtually over two half days and will be immersive, collaborative and stimulating.
